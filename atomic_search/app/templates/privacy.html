What is {{ config.APP_NAME }}?

{{ config.APP_NAME }} is a privacy-focused search engine that allows you to search the web without being tracked, profiled, or monitored. Unlike traditional search engines, we don't collect your personal data, sell your information, or show you targeted ads based on your search history.

✨ Key Privacy Features

🛡️ Zero Tracking

We don't use cookies, analytics, or tracking pixels.

📊 No Profiling

We don't build profiles based on your searches.

🔐 Encrypted

All connections use HTTPS with secure headers.

🚫 No Ads

We don't show ads or sell your attention.

📋 What We Collect

Nothing. That's the short answer. Here's the detailed explanation:

  • ❌ We don't collect IP addresses - Your IP is never logged or stored.
  • ❌ We don't store search queries - Your searches are not recorded.
  • ❌ We don't use cookies - Unless you explicitly enable optional features.
  • ❌ We don't track clicks - We don't know which results you click.
  • ❌ We don't analyze behavior - No patterns, no profiles, nothing.

🔍 How Search Works

When you search on {{ config.APP_NAME }}:

  1. Your search query is sent to our server.
  2. We query our search backend providers.
  3. Results are returned to you with ads/trackers stripped.
  4. The connection is closed. No logs are kept.

🛠️ Optional Features

Some features require minimal data storage:

  • Bookmarks - Stored locally in your browser only
  • Voting - Anonymized vote counts (no personal data)
  • Settings - Stored locally or encrypted

  • ✅ Rate limiting to prevent abuse
